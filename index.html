Plethora

Everything a peripheral

Plethora allows wrapping almost every tile entity as a peripheral. With integration with a wide range of mods, Plethora allows you to interact with the world in new ways.

Fancy modules

Modules expose additional functionality to computers, such as the block scanner which will tell you all about the nearby blocks or the introspection module which allows computers to interface with the player's inventory.

It's not brain surgery

If you've ever wanted to embed a computer in your skull then this is the mod for you! The neural interface combines the portability of a pocket computer and the power of modules, allowing you to directly interact with the player wearing it.

Cyborg farm

Yeah. You can put it on animals too. Combine this with the kinetic augment module and you can control the animal however you want. Skeleton army? Check. Remote control creepers? Check. Your enemies will tremble before your terrifying programming skills.

Lasers

Amongst the other modules included are entity scanners and laser guns. Combine the two and you've got a pretty nifty little mob defense system right there.

One simple request

You know, I have one simple request. And that is to have sharks squid with frickin' laser beams attached to their heads!

Yep. I bet you didn't even know you wanted this until you read it.
